🎵 What is Oriel FX?

Oriel FX is a powerful audio visualizer that creates stunning visual representations of your music. Transform any audio into mesmerizing animations with customizable shapes, colors, and effects.

✨ What can you do?

  • 🎨 Create Visualizations: Upload your music and watch it come to life with dynamic visual effects
  • 🎛️ Customize Everything: Change colors, shapes, pulse intensity, and more
  • 📱 Download & Share: Export your creations as GIF, MP4, or other formats
  • 🚀 Upgrade for More: Get longer recordings, premium formats, and unlimited downloads
